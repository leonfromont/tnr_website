Our Story

Welcome to True North Ritual — a little slice of relaxation, twenty years in the making.

My journey began in the world of pharmaceutical compounding and formulation, where I specialised in anti-ageing protocols. Over time, and through the transformative experience of motherhood, my path evolved naturally into the beauty therapy space. What started as a lifelong love of skincare has become a deep appreciation for how complex — and personal — our skin truly is.

In an industry worth billions, it’s easy to get swept up in the latest trends, glossy packaging, and clever marketing promising instant results. But I’ve learned that truly healthy, resilient skin isn’t found in hype — it’s found in understanding. With transparency, education, and a science-backed approach, my goal is to help you find what genuinely works for you.

You’ll often hear “every body is different.” Well, so is your skin. That’s where The True North Difference lies — a fully customisable skincare solution designed uniquely for you, built on the principles of corneotherapy: working with your skin, not against it.

In a culture that often expects people to push past their natural instincts — whether in parenthood, career, body image, or self-care — True North Ritual offers something different: a place of honesty, safety, and support. Here, you can come exactly as you are, be seen, be heard and be guided toward choices that honour your individual skin and your overall wellbeing.

I work with people of all ages and life stages — from those taking their first steps into skincare to those navigating hormonal, environmental, or age-related changes. Wherever you are on your journey, your skin deserves to be understood, respected, and nurtured.

It's my privilege to be part of that journey. I look forward to meeting you and helping you rediscover your own true north.
