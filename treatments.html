Our Approach to Skin Needling

At True North Ritual, we use the Dermapen™, a medical-grade device designed for precision, consistency, and comfort. Each treatment is tailored to your individual skin goals and condition. Needle depth is adjusted intentionally. Shallower levels refine surface concerns such as pigmentation and texture, while deeper levels stimulate collagen and strengthen skin structure.

Because we want the best possible results, skin preparation is essential. You're welcome to book this treatment online; however, if your skin needs a little more strengthening or balance on the day, we may recommend postponing the needling and performing a restorative treatment instead. This ensures your skin is calm, resilient, and ready to achieve optimal results.

We offer a Skin Needling Essentials Set ($160), which includes nutritional support to optimise your skin health before treatment, along with all the aftercare products you'll need for recovery.
